Help & Support

Find quick answers below, or raise a ticket and we'll get back to you.

🏗️ App not building?

Check your prompt is descriptive enough. Try starting with "Build me a..." and include the purpose, audience, and any style preferences.

🌐 Deployed site not loading?

Cloudflare sites can take 60–90 seconds to propagate after first deploy. GitHub Pages sites can take 2–5 minutes. Refresh and try again.

📦 Package / billing question?

Visit My Account to see your current plan, usage, and expiry. Raise a ticket below if you need a refund or have a billing issue.

🔗 GitHub connect issue?

If your GitHub repos aren't loading, try signing out and signing back in via GitHub. Make sure you authorised Ready4Launch in your GitHub settings.

📄 Document conversion failing?

Make sure your message clearly states the format (e.g. "convert to Word" or "export as Excel"). Attachments must be under 10 MB.

🖼️ Image analysis not working?

Use the paperclip (📎) button to attach your image, then describe what you want to know. Supported: JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP up to 10 MB.
