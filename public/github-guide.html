❓ Do I need to pay for GitHub?

No. The free GitHub plan is all you need. GitHub Pages (which hosts your apps) is completely free on a free account.

❓ What if I already have a GitHub account?

Perfect — just skip Step 1 and click "Connect GitHub" from the sidebar. You'll be taken to the authorise screen (Step 4) and connected in seconds.

❓ What will my app's URL look like?

Your apps will be at yourusername.github.io/app-name. For example, if your GitHub username is priya and you build a portfolio, your URL might be priya.github.io/my-portfolio.

❓ Can I disconnect GitHub later?

Yes. Go to github.com/settings/applications, find "Ready4Launch" and click Revoke. Your previously deployed apps will continue to work.

❓ Something went wrong — what do I do?

Try signing out and signing back in. If the problem persists, raise a support ticket and we'll help you within 24 hours.
